Profit Performance Dashboard

This Profit Performance Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business profitability across time, geography, and product categories. The monthly and month-year profit trends reveal seasonal patterns and fluctuations in revenue, helping identify peak and low-performance periods. The country map and regional pie chart highlight geographical differences in profitability. Product-level insights are shown through the Top 10 Quantity chart and the Top 3 Products bar graph, emphasizing key revenue drivers. The Bottom 5 Cities visualization identifies areas with the weakest profit margins. Finally, the Profit by Category chart compares overall performance across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology.
